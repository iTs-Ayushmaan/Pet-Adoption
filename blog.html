Medical Care for Your Pets

Ensuring your pet's health is vital for their happiness and longevity. Regular vaccinations, dental care, and balanced diets are just the beginning. Always consult with your vet for specific needs based on your pet's age and breed.

  • Core Vaccinations for Dogs: Rabies, Distemper, Parvovirus.
  • Core Vaccinations for Cats: Rabies, Feline Distemper, Feline Herpesvirus.
  • Regular deworming and flea control are essential.
  • Watch for behavioral changes that might indicate illness.

Common Signs of Illness in Pets

It’s important to monitor your pet's behavior and physical health regularly. Here are some signs that may require immediate attention:

  • Loss of appetite or sudden weight loss.
  • Persistent coughing, sneezing, or difficulty breathing.
  • Vomiting or diarrhea lasting more than 24 hours.
  • Lethargy or unusual hiding behavior.
  • Changes in urination habits.

If you notice any of these symptoms, contact your vet promptly for guidance.
