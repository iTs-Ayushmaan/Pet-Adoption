About Us

Making the world a better place for pets and people, one match at a time.

Our Mission

At Pawfect Match, our mission is to connect loving homes with pets in need. Since 2024, we've partnered with shelters across the country to ensure every pet gets a chance at a happy life. By providing support, resources, and a user-friendly adoption process, we aim to reduce pet homelessness and create lasting bonds between pets and their families.
